    "adrenaline" in American English

    See all translations

    adrenalinenoun [ U ]

    us ​ /əˈdren·əl·ən, -ˌin/

    › biology a hormone (= chemical substance) produced by the body when a person is frightened, angry, or excited, which makes the heart beat faster and prepares the body to react to danger
